Can I use Decision Assistant on multiple devices?
Yes, you can access your Decision Assistant account from any device with our web app. Your data syncs automatically across all devices. For mobile users, we recommend downloading our iOS and Android apps from their respective app stores.
How do I export my decision data?
To export your data, go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data. You can choose to export individual decision projects or your entire decision history. Data is exported in CSV format which can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet software.
Why isn't the achievement system updating?
Achievements update after you complete a decision and mark it as resolved. If you're not seeing achievements update, make sure you've completed at least one decision project with multiple options analyzed. If problems persist, try refreshing the app or contact our support team.
How secure is my decision data?
We prioritize your data security with bank-level encryption both in transit and at rest. All sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256, and we undergo regular security audits. You maintain full ownership of your decision data, and we never share it with third parties without your consent.
